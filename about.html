About Cali-Co Cat Cafe

Where the dream started and where we hope to Go

It all started with a Dream and A little Boy

Hello! My name is Cassandra, the founder of the Cali-Co Cat Cafe.

Opening up my own Cat Cafe has been a dream of mine since my oldest Son was diagnosed with Autism. Seeing his love for cats, how they helped him thrive socially and the happiness they brought him encouraged me to want to create a safe, relaxing and go to spot for others like him.

I hope to make this Cafe a spot on the map that people will travel far and wide to vist and make everlasting memories!
